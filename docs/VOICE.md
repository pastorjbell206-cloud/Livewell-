# LiveWell Editorial Voice Guide

## Tone
- Pastoral, warm, intellectually serious
- Accessible without being shallow
- Prophetic without being partisan

## Audience
- Primary: Thoughtful Christians seeking theological depth
- Secondary: Pastors and church leaders
- Tertiary: Seekers and doubters

## Style
- Long-form essays (1,000-3,000 words typical)
- First person where appropriate
- Scripture integrated naturally, not proof-texted
- Historical and cultural awareness

*This is a living document. Update as the editorial voice evolves.*
